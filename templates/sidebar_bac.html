Public mode is faster and more accurate as it uses online APIs. However, it may expose your data and accumulate costs for using the APIs.

Ingesting documents into the local database and tokenizing them is necessary for Private mode. This process may take some time, so feel free to grab a cup of coffee. Public mode is available for quick usage.
